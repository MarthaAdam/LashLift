A lash lift is a cosmetic procedure in which the eyelashes are curled and lifted
up with the help of chemicals. In the case of lash lifts, they are also colored
into a darker shade. No fake extensions are needed in this procedure and
work with natural eyelashes, making them look longer and fuller. If people
want to get a lash lift at home then they must be aware of all steps and also
follow them carefully.
WHAT IS A LASH LIFT?
Lash lifts are also called lash perms. Replace the need for an eyelash curler
completely by shaping and curling your lashes to offer a semi-permanent hold.
According to Starr, this procedure works best for someone who uses a lash
curler daily or has straight natural lashes. It is ideal for someone whose lashes
already have a little bit of length. Excellently, the procedure should be quick
and painless, leaving you with eyelashes you can bat attractive to your hearts
content. Lash lift aftercare is a very important part of the treatment and it is
very simple.
LASH LIFT AFTERCARE
Lash lift and tint aftercare is a very important part of the procedure must avoid
these things in the first 24 hours after the treatment. If you do not follow the
lash lift aftercare instructions properly, there is a chance of lash lift gone
wrong.
1. Do not wear contact lenses
2. Do not get your eyelashes wet
3. Avoid using any products on your lashes
4. Avoid rubbing your eyes
5. Avoid swimming
6. Avoid engaging in activities
7. Do not go to saunas
8. Avoid taking long showers and try to keep your lashes dry
9. Do not sleep on your face
